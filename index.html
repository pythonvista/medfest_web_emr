WHAT IS MEDFEST?

MEDFEST is a one stop multifunctional mobile EMR application/solution that provides an individual a single lifelong EMR account that can only be accessed by qualified health care providers within an interoperable platform of hospitals, pharmaceutical stores, diagnostic structures and virtually all accredited outlets that deal healthcare while still providing privacy, security, and autonomy of patient information, such that there is seamless continuity of patient care anywhere within or without the country regardless that these structures are far apart but they operate electronically as a single entity.
